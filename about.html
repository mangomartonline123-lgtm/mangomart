About Mango Mart

Mango Mart is a hyperlocal e-commerce platform built by four lifelong friends from Srinivaspur—Shashank D Hebbar, Varun Raj S, Nayan Kumar R, and Harish Babu K. Together we are reimagining daily shopping with fresh produce, essential groceries, and stationery delivered with care.

Our Mission

To provide Srinivaspur with a one-stop destination for fresh groceries, fruits, vegetables, and stationery—delivered on time, every time—with a service experience that feels personal and dependable.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted neighbourhood marketplace in Karnataka, expanding thoughtfully while preserving the freshness, integrity, and warmth that define Mango Mart’s roots in Srinivaspur.

Our Core Values

Quality

Innovation

Customer Delight

We go the extra mile to deliver convenience and support that earns long-term trust.

Our Story

Mango Mart began as a shared dream among four friends from Srinivaspur who grew up seeing the challenges local families faced when sourcing quality groceries. We wanted to build a dependable way for our community to order everything from farm-fresh mangoes to classroom stationery—without sacrificing time or freshness.

Starting with early-morning market runs and same-day doorstep deliveries, we built the workflows, technology, and relationships that now power Mango Mart. Every process—inventory planning, packaging, routing, and support—still reflects that hands-on, customer-first ethos.

Today, Mango Mart continues to grow alongside Srinivaspur’s families. We are expanding our catalogue, extending delivery hours, and investing in sustainable sourcing—while keeping our promise of reliability, affordability, and neighbourly service.
