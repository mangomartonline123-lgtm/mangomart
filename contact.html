Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using UPI, credit/debit cards, or cash on delivery.

What are your delivery timings?

We deliver from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. Sunday deliveries run from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Yes! We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM. Orders placed after 2 PM will be delivered the next day.

What if I'm not satisfied with my order?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with your order, contact us within 24 hours and we'll make it right.
